About Me

Background

I began my technical writing career in South Africa, documenting healthcare software. This included a medical information system used by general practitioners, and a laboratory information management system used in pathology labs. Since moving to Australia in 2007, I’ve worked across IT service management, construction project management, and IPTV. My goal is to create clear and practical documentation that helps people do their work more effectively.

Skills

Core Skills

  • Technical Writing & Editing

  • Information Architecture & Content Strategy

  • Infographic Design

  • Generative AI (Custom GPTs, Claude, Gemini)

Tools & Technologies

  • Documentation Tools (Antora, Microsoft Office, VS Code, Confluence)

  • Markup Languages (AsciiDoc, HTML)

  • Version Control (Git, GitHub)

  • Visual Design (Photoshop, DALLE3, Miro)

Experience

  • Created user guides for IPTV by hands-on exploration of software, reviewing specifications, and interviewing developers and business analysts.

  • Maintained and updated technical documentation to support regular software releases.

  • Improved support documentation workflows by incorporating feedback from customer support teams. This resulted in more user-focused content.

  • Partnered with customer support and operations teams to ensure documentation addressed real user needs and reduced repeat queries.

  • Collaborated with marketing to align technical and customer-facing content, creating a consistent brand voice across channels.
